Proposed Gameplay Mechanics

We are considering implementing these concepts to turn Voyager from a simple tracing tool into a tactical, physics-based cosmic navigator. Click the switches above to test their physical interactions!

Physics Hazard

Gravity Wells (Singularities)

Adds gravitational pull centers to the sector layout. Spacecraft must steer actively to avoid being pulled into the core, creating a risk-reward dynamic when stars spawn close to the black hole.

  • Uses real mathematical inverse-square law pull logic:
    F_g = (G * M) / (d² + ε)
  • Requires the pilot to burn Hyper-Boost (SHIFT) to escape deep gravity wells.
  • Visualized with atmospheric distortion waves and a rotating accretion disk.
Environmental Force

Cosmic Solar Winds

Dynamically active ionic currents push your ship off-course in specific directions. Steering feels like correcting a ship in water currents, testing fine maneuvering skills.

  • Constant vector acceleration force applied across the entire canvas grid.
  • Indicated visually by faint, streaming cosmic energy particles.
  • Requires players to master the Retro-Brake (Z) to stop and pivot accurately.
Collision Hazard

Drifting Space Debris

Asteroids drift across the navigation plane. Collisions deplete the ship's biosphere shields, causing sparks, ship bounceback, and score penalties.

  • Dynamic elastic collisions where ship velocity is deflected on impact.
  • Encourages slower, tactical maneuvers or quick, high-risk boosts.
  • Integrates with a new HUD Shield Meter showing biosphere status.
Tactical Utility

Warp Gate Wormholes

Spatially paired gravity anomalies allow instant teleportation. Flying into the Cyan Portal teleports the ship to the Green Portal (and vice versa) while preserving exit velocity.

  • Helps cover long distances quickly in larger constellation grids.
  • Preserves vector speed on exit, allowing pilots to slingshot across sectors.
  • Includes a short recharge matrix window to prevent infinite portal looping.
Immersive System

Web Audio Synth Engine

Fully dynamic, procedural sound generation. Audio is generated directly inside the browser using mathematical oscillator frequencies, meaning no external sound asset lag or file downloads.

  • Engine Humming: Low-frequency Sawtooth/Triangle oscillator with frequency mapped to thrust velocity.
  • Portal Warping: Sweep of a Bandpass filter over white noise.
  • Twinkle Pings: Dual Sine oscillator chords triggered on star visits.
Game Mode

Zen Navigation Mode

A relaxed, score-free mode where players can practice flight mechanics, trace custom constellations, and enjoy the ambient procedural sound synthesis without any time limits or hazards.

  • Disables biosphere depletion timers and cosmic hazards.
  • Allows free tracing of all 12 Zodiac shapes at the player's leisure.
  • Plays a continuous, soothing ambient soundscape chord pad.
