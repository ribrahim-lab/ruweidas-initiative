Our Programs & Projects

Specially curated programs designed to inspire, educate, and equip youth for a tech-driven tomorrow.

5-Day Coding Program

An immersive, hands-on workshop designed to introduce youth to programming logic, web development basics, and software design principles.

Financial Literacy

Equipping young adults with real-world financial skills. Topics cover basic budgeting, the power of compound interest, savings, and investments.

Youth Math Tutoring

Providing direct support to strengthen academic foundations. Focused on building deep confidence in algebra, geometry, calculus, and logic.

Quant Finance & Tech

For advanced learners curious about the intersection of math, coding, and finance. Covers quantitative modeling, algorithmic thinking, and modern tech.

Algorithmic Trading

Learn how math and data fuel modern markets. Introduction to writing basic algorithms that execute simple, data-driven financial trades.

Data Science for Youth

Unlocking the power of data. Youth learn how to gather, clean, analyze, and visualize real-world datasets to solve problems using Python.

Python Game Dev

Applying algebra and geometry in a fun way! Students build their own 2D games while mastering core programming physics and logic.

Fintech & Entrepreneurship

Exploring how digital wallets, smart contracts, and technology are reshaping the future of businesses and global financial systems.
