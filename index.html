About the Founder

Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Ruweida Ibrahim, the founder. I started Ascent Learning Initiative to open doors for youth into the fast-growing worlds of technology, advanced math, and quantitative finance. Growing up, I saw how access to these topics can shape careers, yet they are rarely taught in standard school programs.

Our mission is simple: to make coding, budgeting, algorithms, and mathematical logic approachable and free for every student. Whether you are building your first game in Python or learning how trading models work, we are here to support your journey.

Our Programs & Workshops

A curated suite of projects designed to foster analytical thinking, financial wisdom, and technical mastery.

5-Day Coding Program

An immersive, hands-on workshop introducing programming logic, web development basics, and software design principles.

Financial Literacy

Practical skills for the real world. Covering personal budgeting, compound interest, smart saving habits, and investment basics.

Youth Math Tutoring

Strengthening mathematical foundations. Direct academic coaching in algebra, geometry, trigonometry, and calculus.

Quant Finance & Tech

An introduction to quantitative analysis. Bridging mathematical algorithms, Python scripts, and stock market modeling.

Algorithmic Trading

Learn how math rules Wall Street. Introduce students to writing simple algorithms that analyze historical stock prices and make decisions.

Data Science for Youth

Discover the story behind numbers. Gather, clean, and visualize real-world datasets using charts and Python analysis libraries.

Python Game Dev

Use math to build games! Program game physics, keyboard movement, and collision detection to create interactive 2D projects.
